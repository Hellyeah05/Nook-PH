Frequently Asked Questions

▾ How Do I Place An Order For The Book?

To purchase the book, simply click the Add to Cart button located on the product page. Once the item is added, you can view your cart by selecting the shopping cart icon at the top of the site. From there, click Proceed to Checkout to begin the secure checkout process. You’ll be asked to provide your shipping details, choose your preferred delivery option, and select a payment method (such as credit/debit card, digital wallet, or cash-on-delivery if available in your area). After reviewing your order summary, confirm your purchase by clicking Place Order. Once your order is successfully placed, you’ll receive an order confirmation email containing your order number, item details, and estimated delivery date. You can also track the status of your order anytime by logging into your account and visiting the My Orders section.

▾ How Long Will Delivery Take?

Delivery Information

Standard delivery for your book typically takes 3 - 7 business days, depending on your location and the courier service.

  • Metro areas: Usually delivered within 3 - 5 business days.
  • Provincial or remote areas: May take 5 - 7 business days due to longer transit routes.
  • Peak seasons or holidays: Expect possible delays due to busy courier networks.

Once your order is confirmed and processed, you'll receive an email with your tracking number. You can check updates via the courier’s website or the My Orders section of your account.

Tracking will show when your package is picked up, in transit, and out for delivery.

If you selected express shipping, your order may arrive within 1–3 business days.

▾ What Payment Methods Can I Use?

Payment Options

We want to make buying your book as smooth and flexible as possible, so we’ve set up several convenient ways to pay:

  • Card payments: Use any major debit or credit card. Enter your details at checkout and pay instantly through our secure gateway.
  • Digital wallets: Pay quickly with popular mobile wallets—just a few taps, no card numbers needed.
  • Cash-on-delivery: Prefer to pay when your book arrives? COD is available in selected areas. Hand your payment to the courier upon delivery.
  • Bank transfer: Choose this at checkout to pay directly from your bank account. We’ll send the details and confirm once received.
  • Gift codes or vouchers: Got a promo code or gift voucher? Enter it during checkout to reduce or cover your total.

Every transaction is protected with secure encryption, and you’ll receive a confirmation email once your payment is verified.

If you run into any issues, our support team is ready to help you resolve them quickly.

▾ Can I Return The Book If I Change My Mind?

Book Return Policy

We understand that plans change or tastes shift. You may return your book within 7 days of receiving it, as long as it’s in good condition—cover intact, pages clean, and no markings or damage.

Return Steps

  1. Request a return: Log in to your account, go to My Orders, select the book, and click Request Return.
  2. Prepare the book: Use the original packaging (if available) or wrap securely for transit.
  3. Courier pickup or drop-off: Depending on your location, schedule a pickup or drop off at a designated return point. We’ll guide you through the options.
  4. Refund confirmation: Once we receive and inspect the book, we’ll email you a confirmation. Your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 3–5 business days.

Important Notes

  • Books with damage (torn pages, stains, missing covers) may not qualify for a refund.
  • If you received the wrong book or a defective copy, we’ll cover return shipping and send a replacement at no extra charge.
  • For gift orders, refunds go to the original purchaser.

We want the process to be simple and reassuring, so you can shop with confidence knowing your purchase is flexible.

About Us

NOOK PH. Your cozy corner for great reads.

Why We Exist

We exist because reading should never feel out of reach. Many readers struggle with limited availability, high prices, or lack of options, and our goal is to remove those barriers one book at a time. We want to make it possible for people across the Philippines to find books they love, order them easily, receive them quickly, and choose formats and conditions that fit their needs and budget. Whether someone is a lifelong reader, a student searching for resources, a parent building a child’s library, or a new reader just beginning their journey, they are welcome here.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

NOOK PH’s mission is simple but powerful: to bring joy by making books fast, easy, and accessible with real choices in quality and format for the readers across the Philippines. We believe books are not just items to buy. They are companions. They are teachers. They are safe spaces. They are sparks of imagination, curiosity, and inspiration. And everyone deserves the chance to experience them. So we created a bookshop built around accessibility, flexibility, and genuine care for the reading community, where books are for everyone, not just a select few.

Our Vision

Our vision is a Philippines where books truly reach everyone. We dream of communities where students have the resources they need, families share stories, children grow up surrounded by books, adults rediscover the joy of reading, and books become bridges that connect people rather than barriers that divide access.

When readers choose to shop with us, they support a mission built on respect for readers, passion for books, commitment to service, and growth for the reading community. We value transparency, fair choices, quality standards, accessibility, and reader happiness. For us, it has never been just about selling books — it is about sharing joy, one story at a time.

Whether someone is looking for their next favorite novel, a school requirement, a meaningful gift, or a book that has the power to change their life, we are grateful to be part of that journey. Together with our readers, we hope to make reading open, joyful, and accessible for everyone in the Philippines.

Welcome to NOOK PH — where every story begins.
